# Mobile App

:::warning Early Alpha
The Litara mobile app is in **very early alpha**. Expect rough edges, missing features, and breaking changes between releases. It is not yet suitable for everyday use.
:::

Litara has a companion Android app built with [Expo](https://expo.dev) / React Native. It connects to your existing self-hosted Litara server and lets you browse your library and view book details from your phone.

## Installing

The mobile app is distributed as an APK (Android Package) built via EAS. There is no Play Store listing yet.

1. Download the latest APK from the [GitHub releases page](https://github.com/litara-app/litara/releases).
2. On your Android device, enable **Install from unknown sources** for your browser or file manager.
3. Open the downloaded `.apk` file and follow the on-screen prompts to install.

iOS is not yet available.

## Connecting to your server

When you first open the app you are taken to the **Connect to server** screen. Enter the address of your Litara instance — include the port if it is not on 80 or 443.

Examples:

- `192.168.1.100:3000` (local network)
- `litara.example.com` (behind a reverse proxy)

The app verifies the address is reachable before saving it. Tap **Connect** to proceed to sign in.

To change the server address later, tap your current server URL on the sign-in screen.

## Signing in

<img src={require('@site/static/screenshots/mobile/sign_in.png').default} alt="Sign in screen" width="300" />

Enter the email and password for your Litara account and tap **Sign In**. The same credentials you use on the web app work here.

## Browsing your library

### Dashboard

<img src={require('@site/static/screenshots/mobile/dashboard.png').default} alt="Dashboard" width="300" />

The **Dashboard** tab shows up to three horizontal scroll strips:

- **Reading Queue** — books you've queued to read next, in priority order (only shown when the queue is not empty). Tap **See all** to open the full queue manager.
- **Continue Reading** — books you have started but not finished (only shown when at least one is in progress).
- **Recently Added** — the 20 most recently imported books.

Tap a cover to open the book detail page.

### All Books

<img src={require('@site/static/screenshots/mobile/all_books.png').default} alt="All Books" width="300" />

The **All Books** tab shows your entire library in a two-column grid, sorted alphabetically. Scroll to the bottom to load more books.

- **Tap** a book to open the book detail page.
- **Long-press** a book for a quick-action sheet with options: View Details, Send to Email, Add to Queue / Remove from Queue, and Reset Progress.
- Tap the **grid icon** in the top-right to cycle between 2, 3, and 4-column layouts.

## Book details

<img src={require('@site/static/screenshots/mobile/book_details.png').default} alt="Book Details" width="300" />

The book detail page shows the cover, title, authors, series, description, and metadata (pages, language, publisher, ISBN, Goodreads rating). Genres and tags appear as chips below the description.

The **Files** section lists every file format available for the book. Tap **Download** next to a format to save it to your device's local storage.

## Navigation

The bottom tab bar provides quick access to your main views:

| Tab       | Description                          |
| --------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Dashboard | Recently added and in-progress books |
| All Books | Full library grid                    |
| Search    | Search across your entire library    |
| Libraries | Browse by library folder             |
| Shelves   | Books organised into named shelves   |

Tap the **hamburger menu** (≡) in the top-left on any screen to open the side drawer.

### Side drawer

<img src={require('@site/static/screenshots/mobile/burger_menu.png').default} alt="Side drawer" width="300" />

The side drawer gives access to additional views and account info:

- **Reading Queue** — open the full reading queue screen
- **Series** — browse books grouped by series
- **Annotations** — search and view all your highlights, underlines, bookmarks, and notes
- **Smart Shelves** — automatically populated shelves based on rules
- **Logout** — sign out of the app

The drawer also shows your server version and app version at the bottom.

### Series browser

The **Series** screen lists every series in your library, sorted alphabetically. Each row shows:

- A stacked cover preview (up to two covers layered with a slight offset)
- The series name and contributing authors
- A book count — shown as **owned / total** when a total is known, or just the owned count otherwise

Tap a series to open its **detail screen**, which lists every book in the series in sequence order. Each book shows its sequence number, cover, title, format, publication year, and page count. Tap a book to go straight to its detail page.

## Known limitations

The following features from the web app are not yet available on mobile:

- In-app reader — tap a book to open its details, not to read it directly
- Metadata editing
- OPDS

These are planned for future releases.

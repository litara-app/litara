# Reading Queue

The Reading Queue is your personal "up next" list — an ordered collection of books you plan to read. Unlike shelves (which are unordered groupings), the Queue has a deliberate sequence that you control.

## Adding a book to the queue

Open any book's detail panel and scroll to the **Reading Queue** section in the left column, below the Shelves section. Click **Add to Queue** to place the book at the end of your queue.

The button changes to **In Reading Queue** once the book is added. Click it again to remove the book from the queue.

## Viewing and reordering the queue

The Reading Queue appears as the first section on your **Dashboard**, above "Continue Reading" and "Recently Added".

Each book in the queue is shown as a card. You can reorder the queue in two ways:

- **Drag and drop** — click and drag any card to a new position (desktop)
- **Arrow buttons** — use the left/right arrows that appear below each card to move it one position at a time (works on all devices)

## Removing a book

To remove a book from the queue:

- Click the **In Reading Queue** button in the book detail panel, or
- On the Dashboard queue section, use the arrows until the book is at a convenient position, then open the book detail and remove it from there

On **mobile**, long-press any book and choose **Remove from Queue** from the quick-action sheet.

## Dashboard layout

The Reading Queue section can be hidden or repositioned within the Dashboard layout. Click the **settings icon** (⚙) at the top of the Dashboard to open layout settings, then toggle the Reading Queue section or drag it to a different position in the list.

## Mobile

On the mobile app, the Reading Queue appears as a horizontal scroll strip at the top of the **Dashboard** tab. Tap **See all** to open the full Reading Queue screen, where you can:

- View all queued books in order
- Tap the up/down arrows to reorder
- Tap a book row to go to its detail page
- Swipe or tap the ✕ button to remove a book

To add or remove books from the queue on mobile, long-press any book cover and choose **Add to Queue** or **Remove from Queue** from the action sheet. The action sheet reflects the current queue state, so it always shows the correct option.
